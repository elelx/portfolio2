ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Elva, and I am a Game Design student based in New York City! I love creating 3D critters for my games—tiny, simple creatures that add a sense of escapism. Whether they're roaming around or offering a peaceful break in an intense game, I hope they can bring a touch of calm to balance the action-packed moments.

I’m currently a Freelance artist, crafting short 2D animations, comics, and 3D models. I bring creativity and technical skills to every project. In my portfolio, you’ll find examples of my work, including short games, animations, and personal projects.

I’m eager to collaborate with teams that value creativity and innovation, and to contribute to projects that make players smile.

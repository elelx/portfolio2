The story of Kingdom Aray
story of kings
Visual Novel | 2024
❮ ❯
Programs: Blender, Unity , Adobe Photoshop, Nomad
Role: Solo Developer & Designer
Concept:
This short and whimsical visual novel blends classic fairytale charm with interactive storytelling, inviting players into a magical kingdom where humans and animals live in harmony. In this world, anyone with the right virtues can rise to become king—but only if they make the right choices along the way.
Inspiration:
  • Classic fairytale cartoons – The nostalgic feel of storybooks that flip open as a narrator sets the stage.
  • Cult of the Lamb’s aesthetic – A charming, textured 2D art style that combines storybook warmth with depth and personality.
  • Interactive storytelling – Blending traditional narration with light interactivity to engage the player in unfolding the tale.
    • Gameplay Mechanics
  • Interactive Storytelling – Players experience the narrative by flipping pages, clicking on elements, and uncovering hidden details.
    • Design Goals
  • Immersive Sound Design – Subtle soundscapes and interactive effects enhance the storytelling experience.
  • Rich Visual Texture – Hand-drawn illustrations with layered textures bring the world to life.

Immortalitas Maleditus
cursed immortality
First-Person Horror Exploration Game | 2025
❮ ❯
Programs: Blender, Unity , Adobe Photoshop
Role: Solo Developer & Designer
Concept:
Step into a forgotten museum where the pursuit of immortality took a horrific turn. As you search for your missing father, you’ll uncover the gruesome experiments conducted in the name of eternal life. But some truths should remain buried. A c hilling presence lingers—watching, waiting. Will you escape with the truth, or become part of it?
Inspiration:
This project draws from conspiracy theories, alchemy, and the relentless human pursuit of eternal life. Influences include:
  • Fullmetal Alchemist – The dangers of unchecked ambition and experimentation
  • Secret societies and real-world immortality myths
  • Psychological horror that builds tension through unease, mystery, audio, and visual sensory.
    • Gameplay Mechanics
  • Exploration – Navigate the eerie halls of a long-abandoned museum, searching for hidden secrets and lost research.
  • Puzzles – Solve environmental challenges using found objects, cryptic symbols, and fragmented notes.
  • Narrative – Uncover the dark past of the museum through documents, exhibits, and disturbing visual storytelling.

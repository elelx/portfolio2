NANA and DA's
Walking Simulator | 2025
❮ ❯
Programs: Blender, Unity
Role: Solo Developer & Designer
  • Designed gameplay mechanics, UI/UX, and overall game concept.
  • Developed all aspects of the game, including coding, mechanics, and features.
  • Created and curated all game assets (models, textures, animations, etc.).
    • Concept:
    Nana’s and Da’s is a short walking simulator where players explore one of the two contrasting towns: Nana, the land of animals while Da, the land of vegetables is hidden away. As players interact with the environment, animals, and objects, they uncover the events that led to a rift between the two communities and work toward healing their fractured relationship.
    Key Features:
  • Heartfelt Narrative: The game focuses on the themes of mending relationships and understanding differing perspectives through exploration and environmental storytelling.
  • Low-Poly Aesthetic: The game uses a cozy, low-poly art style to create a relaxed atmosphere while keeping the focus on the narrative.
